ABOUT ME

I'm someone who got curious about data science, fell down the AWS rabbit hole, and somehow ended up loving both equally. I spend my time making sense of messy datasets with Python and SQL, building cloud infrastructure that doesn't fall apart at 2 AM, and occasionally talking to Docker containers like they're stubborn pets.


What excites me? Finding patterns in data that tell a real story, automating things that shouldn't be done manually (looking at you, deployment processes), and that satisfying moment when a CI/CD pipeline actually works on the first try.
